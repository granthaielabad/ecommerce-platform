About Student Mart

Your trusted online marketplace for the QCU community.

Our Mission

At Student Mart, we are dedicated to providing a convenient platform for Quezon City University (QCU) students to buy and sell goods. From homemade products to essential items, Student Mart supports student entrepreneurship and fosters a sense of community.

Our Vision

We aim to create a sustainable marketplace that empowers students to earn, exchange goods, and connect with each other in a supportive environment. We believe in the potential of our community, and we are here to help every student achieve their business goals.

Our Values

  • Community Empowerment
  • Trust and Integrity
  • Supporting Student Entrepreneurship
  • Innovation and Growth
